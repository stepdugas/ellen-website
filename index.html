About Ellen

Ellen Berkman is an Early Career and Career Transition Coach with certifications from the International Coaching Federation (ICF) and the Center for Executive Coaching (CEC). She is dedicated to helping individuals find what works for their unique brain and life circumstances. Her coaching approach blends creativity, empathy, and practical strategy.

Services

ADHD & Executive Function Coaching

Work on time management, prioritization, planning, task initiation, and more — tailored to your brain.

Productivity Coaching

Build efficient routines and accountability systems to get more done with less overwhelm.

Student Success Coaching

Support for academic achievement with tools for focus, time management, and confidence building.

Career Coaching
